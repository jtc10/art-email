Starting on October 12th and running for three weeks, square gallery is proud and excited to display a selection of 6 pieces of the work of Pierre-Auguste Renoir, the French master painter of the great impressionist and Realist movements of the 17th century.

The Works of Pierre-Auguste Renoir:
A Live Tour With Clemence Rousseau

Friday nights at 8:00PM throughout the exhibition, French art critic and Renoir expert Clemence Rousseau will be examining the 6 pieces featured, providing deep insights into Renoir's genius and his beautiful artwork. Register early, as space is limited.
